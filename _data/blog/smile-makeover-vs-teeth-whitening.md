One of the most common questions at Infinity Dental Clinic is: *"Should I just get whitening, or do I need a full smile makeover?"* Both are excellent options — the right choice depends on your specific concerns.

## What Does Teeth Whitening Do?

Professional whitening (in-clinic) uses high-concentration bleaching agents to lighten tooth colour by several shades in a single session. It's quick, affordable, and effective if:

- Your teeth are structurally healthy
- You have **only staining or discolouration** to address
- You're looking for a confidence boost before an event

**Limitation:** Whitening does not fix chips, cracks, gaps, misalignment, or worn-down teeth.

## What is a Smile Makeover?

A Smile Makeover is a customised combination of cosmetic procedures curated specifically for you. It can include:

- **Dental Veneers** — ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth
- **Composite Bonding** — tooth-coloured resin to fix chips and gaps
- **Gum Contouring** — reshaping the gumline for a balanced smile
- **Dental Crowns** — for heavily damaged or discoloured teeth
- **Whitening** — as a finishing touch

## Quick Decision Guide

| Concern | Whitening | Smile Makeover |
|---|---|---|
| Yellowing or stains | ✅ Yes | Optional |
| Chipped teeth | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Gaps between teeth | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Crooked teeth | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Gummy smile | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
