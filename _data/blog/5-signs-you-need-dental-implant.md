Losing a tooth is a bigger deal than most people realise. Beyond the cosmetic impact, a missing tooth creates a cascade of problems — the jaw bone below the gap starts to shrink within weeks, neighbouring teeth begin to shift, and your bite changes.

## The 5 Key Signs

**1. You have a missing tooth or teeth**
This seems obvious, but many patients live with a gap for years. Every month you wait, you lose precious jawbone density that future implants depend on.

**2. Your bridge or denture is uncomfortable**
If an old bridge is causing sore gums, speech problems, or slipping during meals, an implant-supported restoration offers a permanent, stable fix.

**3. Bone loss is showing on your X-ray**
Bone loss is a silent side effect of tooth loss. Dr. Billore may recommend an implant-supported solution along with bone grafting before the loss becomes too advanced.

**4. You're hiding your smile in photos**
Self-confidence is a healthcare issue too. If you're actively hiding a gap or discoloured tooth in photos, a long-term implant solution is worth the investment.

**5. A tooth is too damaged to save**
Sometimes a cracked or severely infected tooth cannot be saved with a Root Canal. In these cases, extraction followed by an implant is the most predictable outcome.
